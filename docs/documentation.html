üìñ Usage Guide

Basic Workflow

  1. 1
    Item Selection

    Choose what you want to craft and set the quantity. Use the search function to quickly find items.

  2. 2
    Configure Bonuses

    Set your skill levels, gear bonuses, and territory fort buffs in Settings for accurate yield calculations.

  3. 3
    Import Inventory

    Use OCR scanning or manual entry to import your current materials and see what you need to gather/buy.

  4. 4
    Analyze Results

    Review the crafting tree and material summary to plan your gathering and purchasing strategy.

Advanced Features

View Modes

  • Net Mode: Shows materials needed after considering your inventory
  • Gross Mode: Shows total materials required regardless of inventory

Summary Modes

  • Material Summary: Lists all required raw materials and quantities
  • XP Summary: Shows experience gained across all tradeskills

Tree Controls

  • Expand/Collapse: Right-click nodes for context menu
  • Bulk Operations: Expand All / Collapse All buttons
  • Remove Nodes: Temporarily hide specific crafting branches
